ABOUT BSUPLA

The Batangas State University Parking Lot App, or, BSUPLA is an application that allows students, faculty, and university employees to manage their parking behaviors more effectively and efficiently. It allows the user to not only automatically detect parking spots at parking spaces, but also reserve parking lots of your choice, as well as notify the user which number is coded or prohibited from entering the premises to park at their designated parking lot. Furthermore, a Batangas State University Parking Lot App, or BSUPLA, must be implemented in order to increase the occupancy rate of the car park, have a better picture of the number of vehicles already present, and thus the number of available spots.
